Story of a Half Sneakerhead

By Victor Liang Zheng Section:MW8F


The term sneakerhead is a person who collects, trades, or admires sneakers as a hobby. I started to form a hobby which is to collect sneakers three years ago. It was when I was sophomore in high school, I realize that I'm in love with sneakers and started to pay attention to it. This happens is when one day, one of my classmates wear a Nike AF-1 into class and start telling the histories of the sneaker he is wearing. It was at that moment, I started to have in contact with the term, Sneakers.

The reason that I say I'm a half sneakerhead is that I don't have the ability to obtain every sneaker that I wanted to and it will be my ultimate dream to obtain every sneaker that I love one day.

There are so many great sneakers that everyone should obtain, those listed are just a few of them. Hope that there will be one day where I can obtain a sneaker without hesitation.
